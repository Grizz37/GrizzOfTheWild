User Feedback and Revisions


I partnered with a few potential "users" for feedback on the navigation and content of my site.


I shared my site with a my mother and my wife. Both users agreed the original readability could use a little help, so I adjusted the font, font-size, and further lessened the hue/saturation levels of my background image. Below is an image that shows changes to both the text layout as well as the navigation of the site.



I also adjusted and fixed audio levels in the video of my PC build project. There was a fan or some sort of noise in the back-ground that made the introductory portion of the video difficult to hear. I leveled the audio in Adobe Premier Pro so that the audio for the portion in question was much more clear.
